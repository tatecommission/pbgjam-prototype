**What will America's forests look like in 10 years? In 50 years?**

**PBGJAM v2: Decision Making Tool**
Funded by NASA and the Pennsylvania Department of Conservation and Natural Resources, this web-based application helps forest managers make optimal planting conditions under a changing climate.
Based on the latest improvements in high definition remote sensing and satellite imagery, climate models, and timber and wildlife data, PBGJAM v2 is an interactive tool to visualize changes in the joint distributions of tree species across the Continental United States.

This application is still under construction as of July 2026.
